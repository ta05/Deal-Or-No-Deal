Deal or No Deal
  • Introduction

Game Instructions

    Before Playing

  • 1. Please log in using your gmail account, if you would like to store your highscore.If not you may continue as a guest. To listen to music you can click the page or the "play button", you will have the option to pause or resume the current track or stop the music entirely.
  • 2. Once you have logged in or continued as guest you will be redirected to the game.

    • Game Rules

  • 1. Choose your initial case. This case will remain with you until the end of the game.
  • 2. After choosing your case you will choose and eliminate 6 cases.
  • 3. The banker will then call you with an offer to buy your case, our game will display the expected value of the offer.
  • 4. You can then choose to accept his offer by clicking on the Deal button, or you can reject him by clicking on the No Deal button.
  • 5. If you select the Deal button, you accept his offer and the game ends. If you select No Deal, the game continues; 2-5 will loop throughout the game.
  • 6. Once there is 1 case left, you will have the option of choosing your case or the final case in the game.
  • 7. After making your decision the case you have chosen will open and display your winnings.
